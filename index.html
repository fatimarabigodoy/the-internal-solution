HERE'S THE THING

Every path through this experience ends the same way: the pitch succeeds. It's not because we rigged the story; it's because Algonquin doesn't train "interns."

They train strategists, designers, copywriters, account coordinators, digital planners, and researchers (among many more!), each one tutored in their craft by professionals still working in the field.

You don't need a generalist who "helps out"; you need a fresh mind that knows what they're doing (and is willing and hoping to learn if they don't).

MEET THE CLASS

While this game shows only six roles, the reality is that every ad student from Algonquin has received training in every area of advertising. Some might specialize in one thing, but we know how it all comes together.

Every agency needs something different, and every advertising student at Algonquin is adaptable.
